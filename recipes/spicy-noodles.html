Description

This recipe is for my current favourite dish: Spicy Ramen Noodles. It's a highly versatile and simple dish to make, the overall recipe of which can be changed massively or fine-tuned in granular detail to provide different flavours and contents that suit your own tastes!

This recipe can be copied wholesale or act as a template to build your own ramen dish from, if you're looking for a little bit of inspiration.

Ingredients

Sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1.5 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoonsn chili-garlic sauce
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 3 tablespoons creamy peanut butter

Noodles:

  • 2 (3oz) packs ramen noodles
  • 2 tablespoons chopped peanuts
  • 2 medium green onions, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Whisk soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, rice vinegar, chili garlic sauce, and ginger together in a small bowl.
  2. Add peanut butter, whisking until well combined, and set aside
  3. Bring 4 cups of water to boil in a pot. Discard the flavour packet and add ramen noodles to the boiling water. Cook until noodles are tender, 4-5 minutes.
  4. Drain noodles, reserving some of the noodle water in case you need to thin out the sauce later.
  5. Pour sauce over ramen noodles, tossing until well coated. If sauce is too thick, thin out with a small amount of the reserved noodle water until you reach your desired consistency.
  6. Garnish with peanuts and green onion, and serve.
