Spicy Pizza

Home

Description

This recipe is for another favourite of mine (you might have a spotted trend): Spicy Pizza that serves 4!

This recipe is remarkably simple, and of course, because it's pizza: toppings can be substituted in and out with complete ease. As such this makes a great recipe to build off of for your own personal tastes!

Ingredients

  • 1 ball pizza dough
  • 0.75 cup pizza sauce
  • 1.5 cup monterey jack (shredded)
  • 3oz sliced ham
  • 1 medium jalapeño

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220. Allow pizza dough to come to room temperature if frozen or chilled.
  2. On a lightly flourd surface, stretch the dough into a 16 inch round base. Spray a perforated pizza tray with non-stick spray and transfer the stretched dough onto the tray.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a one inch area of crust around the ourside edge.
  4. Top with diced ham slices and thinly sliced jalapeño. (Or with toppings of your choosing!)
  5. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Enjoy!
